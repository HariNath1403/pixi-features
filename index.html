The Haldi Ceremony is a vibrant and joyful pre-wedding ritual where family and friends apply turmeric paste to the bride and groom. This tradition is believed to bring good fortune and glowing skin for the big day!

The Mehendi Night is an evening filled with music, dance, and intricate henna designs. Brides often have their hands and feet adorned with beautiful patterns, symbolizing love and prosperity.
